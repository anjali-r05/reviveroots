ReviveRoots is an AI and AR-powered cultural preservation platform designed to help individuals and communities reconnect with their lost heritage, traditions, languages, and cultural identity. In an era of globalization and rapid modernization, countless cultural practices, indigenous languages, folk arts, rituals, and oral histories are gradually disappearing. ReviveRoots addresses this challenge by creating a digital ecosystem that not only preserves cultural knowledge but also actively helps people revive and practice it in their daily lives.
